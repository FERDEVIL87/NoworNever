Now or Never! (2022)
Now or Never! is the opening song of Ultra Galaxy Fight: The Destined Crossroad. The song was recorded in both Japanese and English for the languages' respective dubs of the series. A version that mixed both recordings together was used when the series became available on YouTube. It was first released on CD on July 6, 2022, then again as part of ULTRA GALAXY FIGHT Original Soundtrack on August 3.

Artist: Konomi Suzuki from the Ultra League
Released: 2022
Album: ULTRA GALAXY FIGHT Original Soundtrack
